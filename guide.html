Procurement Management System Guide

Welcome to the Procurement Management System. This guide will help you navigate and use the system effectively.

To get started, log in with your credentials. If you do not have an account, please office supply.

To request item, navigate to the 'home' section and click on 'add to cart'. Fill in the required details and submit the form.

Once the status is approved the item will now ready for pickup
